Description and Functionalities

An App to buy and sell both used and new Pc parts,some of the main features of app are:-

Filters Use advanced filters to get the exact component of your requirements.

Sell Looking for an upgrade??Great, Sell your old part on this app.It can be an upgrade for someone else:)

In app chat Chat with the seller or buyer through the app itself. No need to exchange contact information anymore.

Future features In future I am planning to build a recommendation system for this app where you'll be able to put your budget and other preferences and this app will suggest you perfect combination of components according to your requirnments.
