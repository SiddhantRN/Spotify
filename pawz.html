Pawz

An App that makes rescuing of animals and adoption of rescued animals easier and promotes the awareness of rescuing animals rather than buying them.

Description and Functionalities

This app can be used by both rescue centers and normal people.Some of the main functionalities are :-

Adoption People will be able to post adoptions for any animal they rescued or any rescued animal they might know, and will be able to see nearby animals up for adoption.

Pet Information The PetInfo section will have all the necessary information needed for owning and raising a pet like their training ,medical needs, proper diets etc.

Donations Donations to the rescue centers or animal welfare organisations,people will be able to donate both in terms of money or in terms of supplies needed by these rescue centers.

Animal in need One of the main feature will be that if anyone comes across any animal in medical need then they'll be able to alert about it which the nearby rescue centers will be able to see and then help that animal.
